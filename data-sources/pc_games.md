# 🎮 Top 93 PC Games — 2015 → 2026

> Opinionated PC rankings split by design intent. **Part 1** = Top 56 Single-Player (Non-Indie) · **Part 2** = Top 19 SEA-Viable Multiplayer/Co-op · **Part 3** = 24 Best to Play While Working · **Part 4** = 18 Indie Single-Player · **Part 5** = 18 No-Campaign / Systems & Competitive.
> Covers 2015–2026 releases plus pre-2015 classics still actively updated. **SEA server health** (Indonesia/Singapore, ~20–30 ms to SG) is a hard filter in Part 2.
> **🎨 Visual fidelity filter:** AAA-quality 3D or polished hand-drawn 2D only. Pixel art, sprite-based, ASCII, and stylized low-poly 3D (Outer Wilds family) are excluded — 22 otherwise-deserving titles cut and listed in the appendix.
> **Total ranked = 93.** Parts 3 and 5 are cross-cutting re-orderings of the same titles under a different lens — they don't add to the count.

# 🖥️ PART 3 — 24 Best Games to Play While Working

> Play while you wait — for builds, renders, compile times, or a Claude response beep. The game must tolerate your absence, not demand your attention.
>
> **How this works:** work-friendliness is a filter (pass/fail). Once a game clears it, the same 100-point algorithm decides placement. Scores marked * are estimated for titles not on the main list.
>
> **Visual standard:** discrete GPU required, 3D world or high-fidelity rendered environment — no pixel art, card-only UIs, or ASCII.

### The filter

| Trait | ✅ Passes | ❌ Fails |
|:------|:---------|:--------|
| **Pacing** | Turn-based or fully pauseable | Real-time continuous action |
| **AFK penalty** | None — game waits | Death, match loss, or team grief |
| **Attention window** | Bursts only — think, then commit | Constant reflex input |
| **Connectivity** | Offline / SP viable | Online-only, live session required |

**Column legend:** ⏸️ Pause — instant freeze · 🔄 TB — turn-based, game waits for input · 🛡️ AFK — no death/kick/loss while away (✅ safe · ⚠️ pause first)

## ⚠️ Caveats & Notes

- 🔁 **Remasters & Remakes** judged as new works when the rebuild materially changes the experience (RE2/RE4, Silent Hill 2, Dead Space Remake).
- 🖥️ **PC release date** used when it differs from console (God of War, RDR2, Persona 5R, Ghost of Tsushima, Helldivers 2, Spider-Man 2).
- 🎮 **VR titles** included on merit (Half-Life: Alyx at #21 Part 1) even though they require hardware most PC players do not own.
- 🌐 **Live-service titles** weighted on *current state*, not launch.
- 🧍🤝 **SP vs MP placement** follows *primary design intent* — BG3 supports co-op but is designed around a solo narrative → Part 1. Monster Hunter World can be soloed, but its loop is built around hunts-with-friends → Part 2.
- ♾️ **Pre-2015 classics** share the same 100-point scale as 2015+ releases.
- 📈 **Genre bias** acknowledged: RPGs, soulslikes, and narrative games weight slightly higher on "craft" due to scope.
- 📅 **Provisional scoring for recent releases** — the rubric weights "long-tail Steam reviews (6+ months post-launch)" at 25%. Titles released after Jun 2025 (KCD II, Black Myth Wukong, Silksong, Expedition 33, Indiana Jones, Spider-Man 2, Marvel Rivals, Monster Hunter Wilds, etc.) are scored on launch consensus plus partial tail; expect ±2 drift over the next 6–12 months.
- 🌏 **SEA server ratings** assessed from an Indonesia/Singapore perspective (Jun 2026). Treat 🟡 as variable, especially mid-season.
- ◆ **Indie classification** = independently developed with a small core team; self-published or released through an indie label. Borderline cases marked based on studio culture at release.
- 🎨 **Visual fidelity filter** — applied Jun 2026. AAA-quality 3D or polished hand-drawn 2D only. Pixel art, sprite-based, ASCII, and stylized low-poly 3D (Outer Wilds family) cut, regardless of score. 22 otherwise-deserving games listed in the appendix below. This is taste, not quality.
